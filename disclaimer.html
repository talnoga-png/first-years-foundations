Health Disclaimer

Educational Resources, Not Medical Advice

First Year Foundations guides are educational resources for parents and do not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

About Our Guides

The guides published by First Year Foundations are educational materials designed to support parents in understanding and gently exploring baby development in the first year of life. They are based on the Feldenkrais method and years of hands-on experience with babies and families.

Our guides are not medical instruments, clinical protocols, or therapeutic interventions. They are invitations to observation and gentle movement exploration that complement, rather than replace, professional medical care.

When to Consult a Healthcare Provider

If you have any concerns about your baby's health, development, or wellbeing, please consult with your pediatrician or other qualified healthcare professional. Seek immediate medical attention if your baby experiences:

  • Difficulty feeding or significant weight loss
  • Persistent fever or illness
  • Unusual lethargy or lack of responsiveness
  • Signs of injury or distress
  • Significant developmental delays or concerns
  • Any other symptoms that worry you

Not a Substitute for Professional Care

Our guides do not replace:

  • Regular pediatric check-ups and screenings
  • Professional evaluation by a pediatrician, physical therapist, occupational therapist, or other qualified healthcare provider
  • Specialized assessment and treatment for developmental concerns
  • Medical diagnosis or treatment

Personal Responsibility

The use of First Year Foundations guides is entirely at your discretion and responsibility. You know your baby best. If any activity or suggestion does not feel right for your baby or your family, discontinue it and consult with your healthcare provider if needed.

We encourage you to:

  • Observe your own baby's cues and comfort
  • Stop any activity if your baby seems distressed or uncomfortable
  • Trust your instincts as a parent
  • Consult your pediatrician with any concerns

Assumption of Risk

By using First Year Foundations guides, you acknowledge that baby development and movement activities involve inherent risks. You assume all risk of injury to yourself or your baby that may result from using our guides. We are not liable for any injury or loss that results from the use of our guides or content.
