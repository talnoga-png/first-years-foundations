Why First Year Foundations

A different kind of baby development guide

Most baby development content focuses on whether your baby is hitting milestones. We focus on how to support the movement quality that makes those milestones possible.

Rooted in Feldenkrais

The Feldenkrais Method sees movement as learning. Babies develop through comfort, variation, and freedom not through drills, pressure, or forced positions.

Age-specific, not generic

Each guide is written for one specific stage. You get exactly what's relevant for your baby right now not an overwhelming overview of the whole first year.

No milestone anxiety

We replace milestone charts with an understanding of movement quality. Your baby is not behind. They are unfolding and your job is to make the ground comfortable.

Written by a practitioner

These guides are created by a Feldenkrais-certified practitioner with real experience supporting baby development not by a content team or AI.

About First Year Foundations

First Year Foundations was created by a Feldenkrais-certified practitioner who wanted parents to have access to the kind of developmental understanding that usually only comes from working one-on-one with a specialist.

These guides are educational resources not medical advice, not therapy, not a replacement for your paediatrician. They are a practical, informed companion for the parents doing their best every day.
