Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

First Year Foundations ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and purchase our products.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Purchase a guide: Name, email address, and payment information (processed securely by Whop)
  • Sign up for our free guide: Name and email address (collected through MailerLite)
  • Contact us: Any information you include in emails or messages to hello@first-years-foundations.com

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only collect the information necessary to deliver our products and communicate with you about your purchase.

Automatic Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through Google Analytics, including:

  • Device type and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Traffic source (how you found us)
  • General location information (country/region level)

This information helps us understand how visitors use our website and improve our content. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver your purchased guides and send download links
  • Send confirmation and receipt emails for your purchase
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send educational email content about baby development (if you opt in)
  • Send information about new guides and offerings (if you opt in)
  • Analyze website usage to improve our site and content
  • Comply with legal obligations

Email Communication

When you purchase a guide or sign up for our free guide, you'll receive emails from First Year Foundations (via MailerLite) with educational content and information about our guides. You can unsubscribe from these emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send you, or by contacting us at hello@first-years-foundations.com.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

Whop (Payment Processing) — Processes purchases securely. Your payment information is handled by Whop and subject to their privacy policy.

MailerLite (Email Marketing) — Manages our email subscriber list and sends educational emails. When you sign up for our free guide or marketing emails, your information is stored securely with MailerLite.

Google Analytics — Tracks website usage and visitor behavior to help us improve our site. Google Analytics does not identify individual users.

GitHub Pages (Website Hosting) — Hosts our website. Basic server logs may be collected but are not personally identifiable.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Payment information is processed securely through Whop with industry-standard encryption. Email information is stored securely with MailerLite. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do our best to protect your information, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request that we update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing emails by unsubscribing

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@first-years-foundations.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website and guides are intended for adults (parents and caregivers), not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our website constitutes your acceptance of any changes to this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at hello@first-years-foundations.com.
