What does the 9–12 month guide cover?

The 9–12 Month Guide covers cruising, pulling to stand, first steps readiness, early communication milestones, and balance development for babies aged 9 to 12 months. Independent walking typically emerges between 9 and 15 months this guide helps parents understand walking readiness and how to support it without rushing.

What parents are noticing at this stage

  • My baby is 11 months and not walking yet should I be concerned?
  • They're cruising along furniture but won't let go
  • They only army crawl is that a problem?
  • They fall often when standing
  • I'm not sure what support is helpful versus pushy
  • They can pull up but can't get back down to the floor

Movement protocols include:

  • Balance and squatting the overlooked foundations of confident walking

  • Early communication milestones pointing, waving, and what they signal
