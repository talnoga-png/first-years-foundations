Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our guides and methods

The Feldenkrais method is an educational approach to human movement and learning developed by physicist and martial artist Mosh Feldenkrais. Rather than teaching the "right" way to move, the Feldenkrais method invites exploration of gentle movements and self-awareness. This approach helps your nervous system learn more efficient, comfortable patterns of movement and coordination. For babies and young children, Feldenkrais principles emphasize patient observation and low-pressure environments where your baby's natural development can unfold.

Our guides are informed by the Feldenkrais method, developmental observation, and years of hands-on experience with babies and families. They are educational resources, not medical guidelines. We always encourage you to consult with your pediatrician if you have concerns about your baby's health or development. Our approach complements, rather than replaces, professional medical care.
