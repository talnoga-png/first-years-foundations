What does the 3–6 month guide cover?

The 3–6 Month Guide covers rolling, reaching, grasping, and floor play for babies aged 3 to 6 months. It explains how to support rolling without forcing it, what to do when your baby resists tummy time, and how to help them find their hands and feet through gentle, Feldenkrais-informed movement ideas.

What parents are noticing at this stage
