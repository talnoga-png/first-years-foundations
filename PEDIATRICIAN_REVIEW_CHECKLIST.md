# Pediatrician Review Checklist

Before launch, have a pediatrician review this site for medical accuracy and claim boundaries. Use this checklist to guide their review.

## Pages to Review

**Priority 1 — Educational Content (Core Claims)**
- [ ] `about.html` — Feldenkrais method description, "What we don't do" section
- [ ] `0-3-months.html` — Fourth Trimester concept, nervous system claims
- [ ] `3-6-months.html` — Rolling readiness, movement descriptions
- [ ] `6-9-months.html` — Crawling development, sensory awareness
- [ ] `9-12-months.html` — Standing/walking, coordination development

**Priority 2 — Blog Posts (Educational Narrative)**
- [ ] `blog-tummy-time.html` — "What's actually happening during tummy time" claims
- [ ] `blog-rolling.html` — Rolling development progression, readiness signs

**Priority 3 — Supporting Pages**
- [ ] `faq.html` — Questions 2, 5, 9 (medical advice disclaimers)
- [ ] `disclaimer.html` — Medical claim boundaries, when to consult healthcare

## What to Look For

### ✓ Acceptable Language (Educational, Observational)
- "You might notice..."
- "Your baby might be exploring..."
- "Signs your baby might be ready..."
- "The Feldenkrais method invites..."
- "Your baby's nervous system learns..."
- "Observation can help you..."

### ✗ Flag if Present (Clinical/Medical Claims)
- "Strengthens muscles" (without caveat)
- "Improves coordination" (as definitive claim)
- "Develops balance" (as medical outcome)
- "Prevents delays"
- Any diagnosis-adjacent language: "identifies", "assesses", "evaluates development"
- Any therapeutic claims: "treats", "heals", "corrects", "fixes"
- Age-based milestones presented as requirements vs. observations

## Specific Concerns to Address

1. **Fourth Trimester Concept** (0-3-months.html)
   - Is this terminology accurate and appropriate?
   - Any updates to newborn sensory/neurological science?

2. **Nervous System Language** (throughout)
   - Is "nervous system learning through comfort, variation, freedom" medically sound?
   - Any caveats needed around neurological claims?

3. **Movement Progression** (all guide pages)
   - Are tummy time → rolling → crawling → walking descriptions developmentally accurate?
   - Any safety concerns for the activities suggested?

4. **Refusal vs. Delay** (faq.html, blog-rolling.html)
   - When a baby isn't rolling/crawling on "typical" timeline, what should parents do?
   - Should we recommend professional evaluation thresholds?
