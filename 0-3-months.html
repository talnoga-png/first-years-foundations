What does the 0–3 month guide cover?

The 0–3 Month Guide is a practical, Feldenkrais-informed PDF for parents of newborns. It covers nervous system foundations, gentle tummy time, natural head and neck movement, and how to create a calm movement environment without forcing milestones or overwhelming your baby with stimulation.

What parents are noticing at this stage

You might be wondering:

  • My baby seems to curl up or hold tension is that normal?
  • They always turn their head to one side should I do something?
  • Tummy time feels like a battle am I doing it wrong?
  • I'm not sure how much stimulation is enough
  • How do I know if I'm supporting development in the right way?

These are the exact questions this guide was written to answer.

What's inside the guide

  • 3 movement protocols with step-by-step guidance:
    • Protocol 1: Chest-to-Chest Tummy Time (the most neurologically productive form for newborns)
    • Protocol 2: Gentle Floor Time back lying and brief belly time
    • Protocol 3: Supported Side-Lying
  • Comfort Protocol for gas, fussiness, and unsettled periods
  • How your nervous system shapes your baby's brain
  • Real developmental ranges, not rigid deadlines
  • Calm environment setup and screen guidance
  • Specific signs to watch for and when to seek help
  • Science notes in plain language
