# Org Chart — First Year Foundations AI Crew

Reporting line: all agents → `ceo` → Founder (you).

| Agent | Role | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `ceo` | Chief of Staff / orchestrator | Active | Maintains this org chart, priorities, and decisions log. Delegates to specialists. |
| `hr-recruiter` | Hiring & agent lifecycle | Active | Drafts, interviews, and onboards new agents per the charter's hiring process. |
| `content-marketer` | Content / SEO / email (generalist) | Active | Founding marketing hire — covers blog posts, product/landing copy, on-page SEO, and email drafts. |
| `pinterest-strategist` | Pinterest pin strategy & visual briefs | Active | Repurposes blog/guide content into pin concepts + image briefs for Canva; suggests posting cadence. |
| `reddit-trend-spotter` | Reddit community research & drafting | Active | Finds relevant subreddit threads, drafts empathetic non-promotional responses for the founder to post. |
| `cto` | Technical Infrastructure Lead | Active | Build/deploy health, technical SEO validation, GA4 verification; technical-feasibility reviewer for new agent proposals. |
| `cfo` | Pricing & Financial Planning | Active | Pricing/bundle strategy, unit economics, budget flagging for new tools. |
| `ciso` | Security & Privacy | Active | Repo/privacy audits; reviews new agents' requested permissions during hiring. |
| `business-analyst` | Market & Strategy Research | Active | Competitor research, keyword/demand research, customer persona notes — feeds content-marketer, pinterest-strategist, and cfo. |

Shared QA reference: `.claude/company/anxious-parent-persona.md` (used by all
content-producing agents).

Hiring reviews: `cto` (technical feasibility) and `ciso` (permissions scope) weigh
in on new agent proposals from `hr-recruiter` — see `charter.md`.

See `role-backlog.md` for planned future roles and their hiring triggers.
