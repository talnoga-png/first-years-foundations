# Role Backlog — First Year Foundations AI Crew

Roles identified but not yet hired. `hr-recruiter` works from this list when a
trigger condition is met (or the founder asks directly).

## SEO Specialist

**Trigger:** `content-marketer`'s SEO workload (technical audits, keyword research,
internal linking at scale) starts crowding out content writing.

**Scope:** technical + on-page SEO, keyword research, structured data, internal
linking strategy across product/blog pages.

## Email / CRM Manager

**Trigger:** MailerLite nurture sequences are live and need ongoing iteration
(A/B testing subject lines, segmenting buyers vs. subscribers, new automations).

**Scope:** owns the free-guide → nurture → buyer email lifecycle in MailerLite.

## Social / Pinterest Manager — HIRED 2026-06-14 as `pinterest-strategist`

Trigger met — founder decided to start actively repurposing blog content into
Pinterest pins. See `org-chart.md`.

## Reddit Trend-Spotter — HIRED 2026-06-14 as `reddit-trend-spotter`

Added directly (not via a backlog trigger) — Reddit was already a named target

## Growth & Ops Analyst

**Trigger:** post-launch, once Whop sales + GA4 traffic data exist (2+ weeks of
real data). This is the stats-driven role the founder specifically wants — its
first job is to read real metrics and propose what the company needs next
(new roles, new tools/integrations), via `hr-recruiter` and `ceo`.

**Scope:** weekly/monthly metrics summaries (GA4, Whop, MailerLite), conversion
funnel analysis, proposing new roles or tooling based on evidence.

## Community / Customer Voice

**Trigger:** first real customer feedback/reviews/support questions start coming in.

**Scope:** drafts FAQ updates, testimonial requests, and tone-sensitive
parent-facing replies for founder review.
